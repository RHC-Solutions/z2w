Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

RHC Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Zendesk Offloader application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Configuration Data: Zendesk credentials, Wasabi B2 credentials, and other configuration settings necessary for the application to function.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use the service, including processed tickets, attachments, and execution logs.
  • Session Data: Login session information for authentication purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process and offload Zendesk ticket attachments to Wasabi B2 storage
  • To send reports via configured channels (email, Telegram, Slack)
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your server. We do not transmit your credentials or sensitive data to external servers except as necessary to:

  • Connect to your Zendesk instance
  • Upload files to your Wasabi B2 storage
  • Send reports to your configured notification channels

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the service. Processed ticket records and execution logs are stored in the local database. Log files are automatically archived after 7 days, but you can configure this retention period.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the application
  • Modify or update your configuration data
  • Delete your data by removing the application and database
  • Export your data from the database

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with third-party services including Zendesk, Wasabi B2, email providers, Telegram, and Slack. Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Telegram: @rhcsolutions
  • Website: https://rhcsolutions.com
