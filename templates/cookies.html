Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Zendesk Offloader application uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the application to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Session Management: We use session cookies to maintain your login state and authenticate your access to the admin panel. These cookies are essential for security and cannot be disabled.
  • Security: Cookies help protect against unauthorized access and maintain the security of your session.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session Maintains your login session Session (deleted when browser closes)

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Bootstrap CDN: For loading CSS and JavaScript frameworks. These are necessary for the application's user interface.
  • Bootstrap Icons CDN: For loading icon fonts used throughout the interface.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies that are already stored
  • Block cookies from being set in the future

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent the application from functioning correctly, particularly the login and session management features.

6. Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
